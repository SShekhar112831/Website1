About Me

I am Mrs. Swati Shekhar, a passionate artist and dedicated educator with years of experience in nurturing creativity and academic excellence. My journey in art began at an early age, and I have mastered various art forms including painting, sketching, calligraphy, and traditional crafts.

As an educator, I specialize in Hindi language instruction and handwriting improvement for students from primary to Class X. My teaching methodology combines traditional techniques with modern approaches, ensuring each student receives personalized attention and achieves their full potential.

I believe that art and education are powerful tools for personal growth and self-expression. Through my classes, I aim to inspire creativity, build confidence, and develop essential skills that last a lifetime.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you teach?

I teach students of all age groups, from young children (5+ years) to adults. For academic subjects like Hindi, I focus on students from Class I to Class X.

Do you conduct online classes?

Yes, I offer both online and offline classes. Online classes are conducted via video conferencing platforms with interactive sessions and digital resources.

How long does it take to see improvement in handwriting?

Most students show noticeable improvement within 2-3 weeks of regular practice. Complete transformation typically takes 2-3 months with consistent effort.

What materials are required for art classes?

Basic materials include sketchbooks, pencils, erasers, and paints. I provide a detailed list of required materials before starting classes.

Do you offer group classes or only individual sessions?

I offer both individual and small group classes (maximum 4-5 students) to ensure personalized attention while maintaining an interactive learning environment.
